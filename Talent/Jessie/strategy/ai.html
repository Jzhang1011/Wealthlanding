Decision systems

I use AI and governed knowledge systems to improve decision speed, consistency, and accessibility. My focus is not experimentation for its own sake, but building tools and operating models that help teams interpret information correctly, apply it consistently, and use it in real business decisions.

MIA — Knowledge Access & Decision Support System

Audience: Cross-functional teams needing access to structured marketing and operating knowledge

FOUNDATION

  • Structured operating procedures and knowledge assets
  • Role responsibilities and workflow documentation
  • Index of dashboards, tools, and data locations
  • Pre-structured information for users without direct system access

GOVERNANCE

  • Source citations for traceability
  • No-inference rules to prevent unsupported answers
  • Role-aware responses tailored to user needs

ADOPTION MODEL

  • Training cohorts
  • Ambassador model
  • Office hours
  • Internal communication and onboarding support

EARLY VALUE

  • Improved access to organizational knowledge and data locations
  • Reduced friction for new-hire ramp and self-service learning
  • Created a scalable model for knowledge access without increasing manual support

NEXT

  • Formalize usage analytics (top intents, repeat questions, deflection opportunities)
  • Strengthen content ownership and freshness monitoring
  • Expand use as a decision-support layer, not just an information layer

MMM Decision Support System

Audience: Teams using MMM outputs for investment interpretation and decision-making

FOUNDATION

  • MMM best-practice guidance
  • Role-specific workflows for different decision makers
  • Scenario examples showing how to interpret outputs and act on them

GOVERNANCE

  • Role-based guidance for different stakeholder groups
  • Scenario-based responses for common decision situations
  • Structured use focused on interpretation, not model misuse

ADOPTION MODEL

  • Training cohorts and enablement sessions
  • Learning hub for self-serve education
  • Office hours and guided support
  • Reinforcement through communication and examples

EARLY VALUE

  • Accelerated stakeholder understanding of MMM concepts and decision use cases
  • Improved confidence for teams newer to model-based investment decisions
  • Created a scalable support layer around a complex strategic capability

NEXT

  • Capture decisions influenced to connect usage with investment outcomes
  • Expand scenario library for tradeoff decisions and cross-channel constraints
  • Strengthen integration into planning and investment review rhythms

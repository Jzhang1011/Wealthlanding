A guided retirement design experience

Retirement is not one finish line.

It is a sequence of choices about timing, taxes, healthcare, location, family, and how you want to live. WealthLanding helps you see those choices before they become irreversible.

How retirement really works

Every decision changes the next one.

Most retirement plans begin and end with a savings target. But the same portfolio can create very different lives depending on when income begins, which accounts are used, where you live, and how healthcare is funded.

⏱When you retire

Changes the length of your bridge years, healthcare options, income needs, and conversion window.

🧾How you create income

Changes taxes, ACA eligibility, Medicare surcharges, required distributions, and legacy value.

🌎Where you live

Changes housing, taxes, medical access, daily life, and proximity to people you love.

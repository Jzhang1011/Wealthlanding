How retirement really works

Every decision changes the next one.

Most retirement plans begin and end with a savings target. But the same portfolio can produce very different lives depending on when income starts, which accounts are used, where you live, and how healthcare is funded.

⏱️
When you retire

Changes the length of your bridge years, income needs, healthcare options, and conversion window.

🧾
How you create income

Determines taxes, Medicare surcharges, ACA eligibility, RMD exposure, and legacy value.

🌎
Where you live

Shapes housing costs, state taxes, medical access, lifestyle, and proximity to the people you love.

How retirement really works

Every decision changes the next one.

The same savings can create very different retirements depending on when income starts, which accounts are used, where you live, and how healthcare is funded.

⏱️

When you retire

Changes your bridge years, income needs, healthcare options, and tax-planning window.

🧾

How you create income

Influences taxes, Medicare surcharges, RMD exposure, and legacy value.

🌎

Where you live

Shapes housing costs, state taxes, medical access, lifestyle, and family proximity.

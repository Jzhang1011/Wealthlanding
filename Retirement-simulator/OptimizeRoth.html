How to use this tool

Pick a scenario or customize

Start with one of the four preset paths at the top of the dashboard, or choose ⚙ Customizable path to control every input yourself.

Enter your financial details

Fill in your balances, income, spending, geography, and timeline in the left sidebar. The model re-runs instantly on every change.

Set your conversion strategy

Choose your top federal bracket target and conversion engine. The bracket-leveling engine enforces a smooth annual cap; the optimizer maximizes your chosen objective.

Read the tradeoff center

Scroll to the Tradeoff Center to see which constraints are active, what each costs, and which planning levers can reduce them.

Compare paths in the sensitivity table

The sensitivity analysis table shows six pre-defined paths side by side — Roth at 85, lifetime tax, IRMAA cost, and total conversions.

The four scenario paths

⚙ Customizable path

All inputs unlocked. You set the objective, posture, geography, bracket target, and conversion engine. Best for exploring your specific situation.

U.S. + ACA path

Optimizes for healthcare stability. Conservative conversions that protect ACA subsidies and avoid IRMAA. For those who plan to stay in the U.S. pre-65.

Abroad Roth path

Front-loads conversions before age 63 during an abroad window with no state tax. Maximizes the cheapest conversion years before Medicare lookback begins.

Tax efficient path

Bracket-leveling engine with a 24% cap — smooth, disciplined conversions every year without spiky brackets. Best for long-horizon steady conversion.
